## Additional Steps

1. **Google Safe Browsing:** https://safebrowsing.google.com/safebrowsing/report_error/?hl=en
2. **VirusTotal:** Re-scan your URL periodically at https://www.virustotal.com/gui/home/url
3. **Enable DNSSEC** at your DNS provider to strengthen domain reputation
4. **Verify server-side integrity** — check that no malware is injected at the hosting level (especially if using shared hosting)
