People first

We put our people first. It's something a lot of companies say, but not a lot of companies do. Here at Performalytic, it's our most important core value. We believe when we place a high value on our team's happiness, work/life balance, and professional development, everybody wins—clients included.

Performalytic is a place to grow

Performalytic is rapidly expanding nationwide, both organically and through partnerships with like-minded technology and managed service providers. We are building a strong network to deliver consistently exceptional service to our clients.

Even as Performalytic grows, we maintain a close-knit, agile culture. You'll work in fast-moving teams, make a real impact for our clients, and contribute meaningfully every day. When you're ready to grow, Performalytic grows with you.
