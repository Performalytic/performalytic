Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on the Performalytic website.

Last updated: July 19, 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve your experience. Cookies may be set by us (first-party) or by third-party services we use (third-party).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies — Required for the website to function properly. These enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand how visitors interact with our site by collecting anonymous information about pages visited, time spent, and error messages.
  • Preference Cookies — Remember your settings and preferences to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Marketing Cookies — Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Cookies We Use

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information such as page views, session duration, and referral sources. This data helps us improve our content and user experience. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Tawk.to Live Chat

We use Tawk.to to provide live chat support. Tawk.to may set cookies to remember chat history and preferences. For more information, see Tawk.to Privacy Policy.

Google Ads & Remarketing

We may use Google Ads and remarketing tags to show relevant advertisements to visitors who have previously visited our site. These cookies track browsing behavior across websites.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings — Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Check your browser's help section for instructions.
  • Opt-Out Tools — You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
  • Cookie Consent Banner — When you first visit our site, a banner allows you to accept or customize your cookie preferences.

Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Cookies

Some pages on our site may embed content from third-party services (e.g., Google Docs, YouTube, social media platforms). These services may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies, and you should check the relevant third party's privacy policy for more information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at support@performalytic.com or visit our Contact page.
