How we calculate your ROI

Our methodology is based on peer-reviewed research and industry benchmarks from leading analyst firms.

Cost of Poor Data Quality

IBM research estimates that poor data quality costs US organizations $3.1 trillion annually. Harvard Business Review reports that 25% of revenue is lost due to bad data.

We apply these benchmarks to your organization's specific revenue, headcount, and industry to calculate your personalized cost estimate.

  • IBM: Poor data quality costs $2.5-3.5 trillion annually in the US
  • Gartner: Average organization loses $12.9M per year from bad data
  • HBR: 25% of revenue lost to poor data quality

Data Quality Improvement ROI

Research from Stanford University and MIT shows that data quality improvements deliver 3-5x ROI within the first year. We factor in implementation costs and ongoing savings.

  • Stanford/MIT: 3-5x ROI on data quality investments
  • McKinsey: 15-25% cost reduction with proper data governance
  • Forrester: 20% increase in revenue from data-driven decisions

Note: This calculator provides estimates based on industry averages. Actual results depend on your specific data challenges and implementation quality.
